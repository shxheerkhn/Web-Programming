PayPal for business

Accept payments online, in person and on the move with tools that fit the way your business works.

Ways to accept payments

On your website

Add PayPal buttons so customers can pay with their balance, cards or local methods they already know.

Send invoices

Create simple invoices from your PayPal account and let customers pay you online in a few clicks.

Payment links

Share a link in chat, email or social media so people can pay you without visiting a website.

Set up a business account

  1. Sign up for a PayPal business account with your work email.
  2. Add your business details and confirm your ownership.
  3. Connect your bank account so you can withdraw payments.
