Resolution Center

The Resolution Center is where you can report a problem with a purchase or a payment.

  1. Find the payment in your activity that you want to report.
  2. Choose the issue that best matches your problem.
  3. Work with the seller and PayPal to try to solve it.

Contact us

If you cannot find what you need in the Help Center or Resolution Center, you can contact PayPal.

  • Send a message after you log in so PayPal can see your account details.
  • Check if phone support is available in your region.
  • Look at the PayPal community for tips from other customers.
