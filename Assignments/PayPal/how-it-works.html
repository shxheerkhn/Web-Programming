How PayPal works

PayPal helps you move your money in a few simple steps, whether you are shopping, sending, or getting paid.

Getting started

1

Create your PayPal account

Sign up with your email, choose a password and add your basic details.

2

Link a way to pay

Connect your bank account or cards so you can send money and shop online.

3

Use PayPal at checkout

When you see the PayPal button, log in and choose how you want to pay.

What you can do

  • Send money to friends and family using their email address.
  • Receive payments when you sell items or complete work.
  • Keep track of your activity, balances and linked payment methods.
