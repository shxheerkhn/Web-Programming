How PayWave works

PayWave lets you move money using your email address and a password instead of entering your card details every time.

Three simple steps

1

Create your account

Sign up with your email address and a secure password. Add a few personal details to get started.

2

Add a way to pay

Link your bank account or cards to your PayWave account so you can send and receive money.

3

Use PayWave online

Choose PayWave at checkout, send money to people you know or get paid for the things you sell.
